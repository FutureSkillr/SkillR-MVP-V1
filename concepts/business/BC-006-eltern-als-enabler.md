# BC-006: Eltern als Enabler und Gatekeeper

**Status:** draft
**Created:** 2026-02-17

## Concept
Parents are the gatekeepers of youth engagement and the financial enablers of the platform. While the student is the primary user, the parent decides whether the app gets installed, how much time is allowed, and eventually whether a premium tier is worth paying for.

The parent value proposition is unique: "Endlich verstehe ich, was mein Kind auf dem Handy macht — und es ist sinnvoll."

### Parent Journey
1. **Awareness** — Teacher, Arbeitsamt, or other parent recommends Future Skiller
2. **Evaluation** — Parent checks what the app does, sees demo/explanation
3. **Enablement** — Parent allows child to use the app (device, time)
4. **Monitoring** — Parent checks progress dashboard periodically
5. **Celebration** — Parent sees milestones, discusses interests with child
6. **Upgrade** — Parent considers premium features for deeper profile/matching

### Revenue Angles
- **Freemium youth tier**: VUCA journey is free, funded by chamber/company sponsoring
- **Parent premium tier**: Enhanced progress dashboard, detailed interest analytics, multi-child management
- **Family package**: Combined parent + youth access with shared milestone tracking

## Value Proposition
- **Parents** gain transparency and peace of mind ("mein Kind verschwendet nicht seine Zeit")
- **Platform** gains a paying customer segment (parents control purchasing for youth)
- **Students** benefit from parental support and encouragement (Motivator role)

## Revenue / Impact
- Parent premium subscriptions as B2C revenue stream
- Parents as word-of-mouth multipliers (BC-008)
- Parental engagement correlates with student retention (indirect value)

## Risks
- Privacy tension: parents want to see everything, students want privacy → must enforce clear boundaries
- Over-monitoring kills intrinsic motivation — dashboard must show clusters, not conversations
- GDPR for minors: parental consent required for under-16 data processing in Germany
- Parents may push career agenda → app must reinforce "Interessenprofil, nicht Berufsempfehlung"
