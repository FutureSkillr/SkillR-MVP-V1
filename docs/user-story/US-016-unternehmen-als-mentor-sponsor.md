# US-016: Unternehmen als Mentor und Sponsor im VUCA-Ökosystem

**Created:** 2026-02-17

## User Story
As a **company**, I want to actively contribute real-world context and sponsored learning experiences to the VUCA journey so that **students experience my industry as a living possibility — and a match can emerge naturally from genuine interest, not from a job listing**.

## Context
The transcript establishes that companies must be active participants, not passive listing providers. "Wir nehmen die Firmen in die Verantwortung — sie muessen um dabei zu sein und um attraktiv zu sein den jungen Menschen eine Perspektive, also Ihren Moeglichkeitsraum aufzeigen."

In the VUCA-Lernwelt, companies have a dual role:

### Mentor
- Provide authentic expertise that enriches the learning journey
- A Nescafé specialist explains coffee cultivation in a journey through Brazil
- A forestry company contributes context when a student explores "Holz" in Canada
- This is NOT advertising — it's educational sponsoring that creates genuine value

### Sponsor
- Companies pay to provide sponsored content/experiences within relevant journeys
- Transparent: student knows the context is sponsored ("Supported by...")
- Revenue model: companies pay for visibility through educational contribution
- "Wir schaffen Sichtbarkeit fuer das Unternehmen, weil du als Unternehmen eine Expertise hast, die unseren Kunden nuetzt" (transcript)

Companies can also register **non-standard positions** — new apprenticeship concepts that don't yet exist in the IHK catalog. This future-proofs the matching engine and reflects the VUCA reality that new roles emerge constantly.

## Acceptance Indicators
- A company can create and maintain their Moeglichkeitsraum profile (FR-022)
- A company can sponsor relevant content that appears in student journeys (FR-028)
- Sponsored content is transparently labeled but educationally valuable
- Students who explore a company's domain can discover that company naturally
- A match can emerge when student interest + company Moeglichkeitsraum align
- The company says: "Ich sehe, wie Matching hier funktionieren koennte" (Checkliste.md)
